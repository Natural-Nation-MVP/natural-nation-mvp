# KB-FOUNDER-006 — AI Operations

Status: Founder Approved

## Purpose

AI Operations coordinates the AI workforce inside Founder OS. It shows who is responsible for what, which handoffs are active, and which operating standards must be followed before and after AI work.

## First Runtime Scope

AI Operations currently surfaces:

- AI agent registry
- role responsibility
- current agent status
- active handoff queue
- operating standards
- approval and synchronization requirements

## AI Roles

- Art: architecture, systems, standards, and ADRs
- Codex: implementation and technical delivery
- Gemini: design review and UX validation
- GPose: prompts, summaries, planning, and founder-facing documentation
- Duey: wellness knowledge and mentor behavior
- Founder: final approval for locked decisions
