# KB-PRODUCT-001 — Natural Nation Product

Status: Founder Approved

## Purpose

Natural Nation is a wellness platform that helps people make their wellness goals a reality through personalized daily guidance, protocols, progress tracking, and the Duey AI wellness mentor.

## Product Direction

Natural Nation is mobile-first and focused on daily action, consistency, habit formation, education, and simple progress visibility.

## Core MVP Areas

- Daily dashboard
- Personalized onboarding
- Duey mentor guidance
- Daily wellness protocol
- Wellness Score
- Rejuvenation Score
- Progress tracking
- Protocol assignment

## Approved Principles

- Duey is a mentor, not a medical provider.
- Guest First architecture remains active.
- Predictive AI, wearables, biometrics, and large-data automation are Phase 2 unless explicitly approved.
- Wellness guidance must be simple, actionable, and non-diagnostic.
