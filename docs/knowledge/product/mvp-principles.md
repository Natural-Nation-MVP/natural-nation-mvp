# KB-PRODUCT-002 — Natural Nation MVP Principles

Status: Founder Approved

## Purpose

This document records the approved Natural Nation MVP product principles.

## Product Promise

Natural Nation helps people make their wellness goals a reality through clear daily action, simple education, protocol consistency, and supportive mentor guidance.

## MVP Focus

The MVP should deliver value quickly during the first session and first several days.

The app should prioritize daily use, clarity, and habit formation over complex analytics.

## Core MVP Features

- onboarding
- personalized daily dashboard
- Duey mentor guidance
- protocol timeline
- progress tracking
- Wellness Score
- Rejuvenation Score

## Locked MVP Rules

- Duey is a mentor, not a coach.
- Duey is never human.
- Guest First architecture remains active.
- Shopping is not part of the current bottom navigation.
- Predictive analytics, wearables, biometrics, and large-data automation are Phase 2 unless explicitly approved.
